About S5S

Stage 5 Staging takes great pride in offering the finest in modular, lightweight, portable performance stage platforms, seating risers and accessories to customers worldwide. In addition to our portable staging products, which are all designed and manufactured in‐house, we also have a custom ornamental rail division, Railing Concepts, that engineers, manufactures and installs rails to your design concepts. We have provided superior product solutions for stadiums, arenas, ballparks, convention centers, theatres and production companies, as well as educational, worship and governmental organizations, since 1990.

Our company and staff designers continuously consult designers and architects around the world, ensuring that we are always at the forefront of technology and that our products are second to none. And because our in‐house design team consists of many seasoned engineers, we have the expertise and know‐how to produce the most superior and advanced modular, lightweight, custom designs that perfectly fit our clients’ needs every time.
